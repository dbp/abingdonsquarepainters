We are a cooperative, a space where abstract, representational, and conceptual artists can work. We invite you to browse this site, get acquainted with the work of our members and hope to meet you at our annual exhibition or Long Island City Open Studios!

News and Events

  • 23.3.2012

    On May 19 and 20 2012 Abingdon Square Painters will participate in the third annual Open Studios LIC. The Open Studios is now part of Long Island City Arts Open - an expanded arts festival that includes Visual Arts, Theatre, Music, Dance and many other activities

  • 24.1.2010

    Welcome to our new Studio!

    The Abingdon Square Painters completed its move to Long Island City on January 16th 2009. The move went very smoothly and the space seems to fit the needs of the members very well. It is hoped that new and exciting things are in store for all of us in this new location!
